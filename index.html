my favourite food is marrocain tagine

the ingredients needed

  • Tbs olive oil
  • Carrots
  • Round Zucchinis
  • Zucchinis
  • Cauliflour
  • Onions
  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Olives

the steps you need

  1. Cut the vegetables the same size, heat the olive oil, and add carrots.
  2. Add the two zucchinis, cauliflour and the chopped onions.
  3. Potatoes, green bell peppers, and tomatoes cut in slices.
  4. Add the spices, let cook 45 min without adding water
  5. Add olives 10 min before turning of the heat
